About Us

SoDrO (Soft Drinks Organizer) is a website created by 3 Computer Science College students (Fillet-O-Fish team) which aims to manage the preferences when it comes to non-alcoholic drinks for both a user and a group of users. The app's documenation can be found
here!

•

Why choose our website?

Nowadays, there's a very common spell which says "You are what you eat". Well, we think that the same thing can be applied in terms of drinks. Taking into consideration that there are plenty of chemicals in both food and drinks, it is of great importance to know what exactly they contain and whether they could be a threaten for our health as we consume them daily.
Our website is focused only on providig information about drinks, from any allergen and ingredients each drink has to recommendations based on your geographical region, availability, expiration time and so on. Not only that, but we also provide the posibility of creating a customized shopping list which will include all the drinks you plan to buy and consume in the forseeable future.
Last but not least, all users will have access to a leaderboard that includes the highest-ranked drinks according to the current statistics.
